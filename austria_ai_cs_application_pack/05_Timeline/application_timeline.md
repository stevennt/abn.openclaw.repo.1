# Application Timeline (Grade 11 → University Entry)

## Grade 11 (Now)
- Build strong profile: grades, projects, competitions
- Start CV and personal statement drafts
- Research Austrian universities + language requirements

## Summer Before Grade 12
- Finalize university shortlist
- Request recommendation letters early
- Prepare language tests (IELTS/TOEFL/German if needed)

## Grade 12 – Semester 1
- Tailor motivation letters per university
- Gather official transcripts + notarized translations if required
- Prepare passport and identity documents

## Grade 12 – Semester 2
- Submit applications before deadlines
- Follow up missing documents
- Prepare for potential interviews/assessments

## After Offers
- Compare offer conditions
- Confirm enrollment
- Begin visa/residence and accommodation process
